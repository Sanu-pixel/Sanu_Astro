Helpful Resources

A curated list of external websites, tools, and software useful for amateur astronomers and space enthusiasts.

  • NASA Official Website

    nasa.gov

    Latest news, mission updates, images, and educational content from NASA.

  • Heavens-Above

    heavens-above.com

    Predict satellite passes (ISS, Starlink), Iridium flares, and view interactive sky charts for your location.

  • Stellarium Web Online Star Map

    stellarium-web.org

    A realistic online planetarium in your browser. Excellent for identifying stars, planets, and constellations.

  • Sky & Telescope Magazine

    skyandtelescope.org

    Reputable source for astronomy news, observing guides, and equipment reviews.

About Stellar Horizons Society

Our Mission

Founded in 2010, the Stellar Horizons Society is dedicated to fostering a community of space lovers, amateur astronomers, and sci-fi dreamers. We aim to inspire people of all ages to look up, explore the universe, and appreciate our place within it through education, observation, and imagination.
